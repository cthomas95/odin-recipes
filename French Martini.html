Description

This chic and sophisticated drink is not just a cocktail, itâ€™s an experience. With its velvety blend of vodka, Chambord, and a splash of pineapple juice, this martini is a perfect fusion of sweet and tart.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1 oz pineapple juice
  • 1/4 oz Chambord raspberry liqueur
  • ice

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients, including the ice, in a shaker.
  2. Shake vigorously.
  3. Strain and pour into a martini glass. Enjoy!
